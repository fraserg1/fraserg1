## Projects

### 1. Weather Prediction

- **Repository:** [Notebook](https://github.com/fraserg1/weather-prediction) | [Project Notes](https://github.com/fraserg1/weather-prediction/blob/main/Weather_Prediction.ipynb)
- **Brief Description:** Built a multi-class classification model to predict weather types such as rain, drizzle, fog, sun, and snow using meteorological measurements.
- **Tools & Skills Used:** Python, Pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn, LabelEncoder, Min-Max Scaling, Matplotlib, SMOTE, GridSearchCV
- **Project Goals:** Predict weather categories accurately while handling class imbalance and improving model generalization.
- **Results:** Achieved an F1-macro score of 57% using a Random Forest model with SMOTE and hyperparameter tuning.

### 2. Wine Quality Prediction

- **Repository:** [Notebook](https://github.com/fraserg1/wine-quality-prediction/blob/main/Wine_Quality.ipynb) | [Project Notes](https://github.com/fraserg1/wine-quality-prediction)
- **Brief Description:** Developed a classification model to predict whether wine quality is good or bad based on physicochemical features.
- **Tools & Skills Used:** Python, Pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn, SVM, SMOTE, StandardScaler, GridSearchCV, Seaborn
- **Project Goals:** Identify the most influential wine characteristics and build a reliable classification model.
- **Results:** Achieved 91% accuracy and an F1-macro score of 79% with an optimized SVM model.

### 3. Stock Price Prediction (Tesla)

- **Repository:** [Notebook](project_3__Stock_Prediction.ipynb) | [Project Notes](https://github.com/fraserg1/weather-prediction)
- **Brief Description:** Built an LSTM-based deep learning model to forecast Tesla stock prices using historical OHLCV time-series data.
- **Tools & Skills Used:** Python, TensorFlow, Keras, LSTM, Pandas, NumPy, MinMaxScaler, Plotly, Matplotlib
- **Project Goals:** Capture sequential patterns in stock movement and generate meaningful future price predictions.
- **Results:** Achieved a MAPE of 5.45% with a well-structured LSTM model and time-series preprocessing pipeline.

### 4. Sales Analysis Reporting, Dashboard & Forecasting

- **Repository:** [Full Report](https://github.com/fraserg1/sales-analysis-reporting-and-forecasting/blob/main/Sales%20channels%20analysis%20report.pdf) | [Project Notes](https://github.com/fraserg1/sales-analysis-reporting-and-forecasting)
- **Brief Description:** A full project analysing the drivers of sales and changing trends for a global manufacturer. Includes a dyanamic BI Dashboard, Market Basket Analysis and Sales Forecasting 
- **Tools & Skills Used:** SQL, Tableau, PowerBI, Python, Pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn
- **Project Goals:** Generate insights and recommendations to help understand the key trends and drivers of sales for the business across it's different sales channels globaly.
- **Results:** Discovered that a clear shift in sales towards online occured in 2013, where number of transactions increased exponentially by +370%
